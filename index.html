AP's MARSANIA

From near and far, those who have found the universe of tomorrow have rejoiced many projects that have promised them a bright future…

Beginning my quest, I came from a small town called Audiana. My ancestors, those who originally and rightfully founded the great land of Marsania, ended up as farmers living their old age out in the middle of nowhere. Their power was usurped by the non-believers, and it became my sole mission to restore the balance of power once again to its natural state.

The new king, Hadeous, was a torturerer, enslaver, and a coward of a king. He took control of the kingdom’s history and erased my clan’s name. But, I fixed that. I lifted the Sword of Mars and used it to slay him where he sat and watched my beloved land fall. I took his throne, looked at it, and threw it into the Royal Fire and watched it burn to its core. The toll it took from the citizens of Marsania; the sapphires on the base that agonized fathers mined, the rubies on the arch that tormented mothers were coerced to do, the emeralds the helpless young were trained to uncover; all of it was finally over. Marsania accepted me as their leader at that moment.

But, this role of King comes with a level of responsibility that I HAVE to uphold. Bringing glory back to the land, I want to create solidarity and inspire trust and faith throughout the community. Here, we create multiple Collections of NFTs, and a significant portion (15%) of ALL profits will be donated to the Princeton Foundation for Peace and Learning (pfplus.org).

We also aim to establish maximum utility for all of our consumers; owning our art is more than just a “flex.” Being even a citizen, will entail you to knowledge from the archives of the kingdom, and counsels with me, myself.

What’s an NFT?

An NFT stands for “Non-fungible token” and is a fancy way of saying it’s a unique, one of a kind digital item that users can buy, own, and trade. Some NFTs main function are to be digital art and look cool, some offer additional utility like exclusive access to websites or participation in an event, think of it like a rare piece of art that can also act as a “members”card. What is Metamask? Metamask is a crypto wallet that can store your Ethereum, and is needed to purchase and mint a NFT. Having a wallet gives you an Ethereum address (i.e. 0xABCD….1234), this is where your NFT will be stored. Learn more about Metamask and how easy it is to use over here!

DAO Implementation

Many of you have asked about the DAO. While the DAO is a great concept, it is nearly impossible to execute efficiently, from tax issues to just having to vote over everything. This is NOT what Audemars wanted. We removed this for the pure fact of sustainability. We knew a DAO would quite frankly just lead to headaches. Imagine if you had the entire DAO to vote to spend all of the company's money on a video game. Now, let's say this game would have lead to failure. If we used a DAO concept the owners would have no power to stop this.
